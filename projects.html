Unpopuhorroropinion

Solo Project

Unpopunhorroropinion is a site where horror lovers can go to catalog their collection and share photos of their collection with other horror lovers. It is meant to not only be a catalog of what films you own, but also a place to share the love of horror. Along with the ability of reviewing your favorite, or most hated horror films in your collection!

Deadreads

Group Project

Deadreads, a goodreads clone, is a social media application that allows users to review horror books, add horror books to bookcrypts along with, view reviews and profiles of other horror book lovers. Deadreads was made using Javascript, Express, and Pug.

Monster Dodge

Unity Test

Monster Dodge is a game I made using Unity and C# before beginning App Academy via a Udemy Course. It was the second game that I made using Unity. It is a simple game where you try to survive while dodging monsters falling from the sky. It was a fun project and a nice sort of intro to Unity. The art in the game was drawn digitally by me.
